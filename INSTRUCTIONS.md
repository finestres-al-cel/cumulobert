Usage instructions:
1. Open a new terminal
2. Go to cumulobert `bin` folder:
    `cd <path to your cumulobert repo>/bin`
2. Load your conda environment (see README.md):
    `conda activate my_cumulobert_env`
3. Open the cumulobert app to extract the photometry of your cluster
    `python cumulobert_app.py`
4. Open jupyter lab
    `jupyter-lab`
5. Open the CumulObert.ipynb file and finish its instructions to finish the analysis
